On-Grid Solar Systems

We specialize in On-Grid Solar System solutions designed to power homes, businesses, and industries while significantly reducing electricity costs. An on-grid solar system is connected directly to the utility grid, allowing you to use solar energy during the day and export excess power back to the grid for credit through net metering.

Our expert team handles the complete process — from system design and installation to commissioning and government approvals — ensuring a smooth and efficient transition to solar energy.

Key Features of On-Grid Solar Systems:

  • ✅ Connected to Utility Grid for seamless energy flow
  • ✅ Net Metering available to earn credits on surplus power
  • ✅ No Battery Required, making it cost-effective and low maintenance
  • ✅ High-Efficiency Solar Panels & Quality Grid-Tie Inverters
  • ✅ Custom Sizes for residential, commercial, and industrial use
  • ✅ Government Subsidy & Tax Benefits available (as per policy)

Our Services Include:

  • ✅ Site survey & energy assessment
  • ✅ System design with accurate load calculations
  • ✅ Installation using premium panels and inverters
  • ✅ Grid connection & net metering approval
  • ✅ Testing, commissioning & after-sales support
