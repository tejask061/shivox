About Us

At Shivox, we are dedicated to delivering advanced solar and electrical solutions that contribute to a more sustainable future. As a trusted provider, we offer a comprehensive portfolio of high-quality products, including solar panels, solar inverters, batteries, earthing kits, APFC panels, MCC panels, electrical panels, and more.

Our expertise covers a wide range of systems, including on-grid, off-grid, and hybrid solar solutions, tailored to meet the specific needs of residential, commercial, and industrial clients. Each solution is designed to maximize efficiency, reliability, and long-term value, making clean energy both accessible and cost-effective.

Beyond products, Shivox provides end-to-end services, including installation, maintenance, commissioning, and ongoing support, ensuring that every system operates at peak performance. Our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and sustainable practices has positioned us as a trusted name in the energy sector.
